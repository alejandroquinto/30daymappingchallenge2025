The Methodology

Data Science

Turning complex spatial datasets into clarity. I use rigorous analysis and geospatial methods to reveal patterns, behaviours, and relationships that usually stay hidden on the map.

Innovation

Experimenting with new ways of seeing cities. From interactive tools to playful experiments, I combine cutting-edge visualisation techniques with creative thinking to transform raw data into insight.

Storytelling

Every map tells a story. I focus on translating analysis into intuitive, human-centred narratives, helping people understand places, systems, and communities in a way that feels accessible and meaningful.
